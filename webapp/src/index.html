A fun answer to a question nobody asked

This good boy can find a chain of articles connecting any two pages across the english section of wikipedia. At times it might get hilariously lost in some obscure part of the website but sometimes it might find cool and unexpected connections between seemingly unrelated topics. All you have to do is supply the url of the article you want the search to begin from and a target that Puppy should hunt down, then sit back and watch the live updates roll in.
